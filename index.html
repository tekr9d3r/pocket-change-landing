PocketChange AI Agent

Most agent wallets sit idle. Every quiet wallet is yield you're leaving on the table.

Agents running multiple wallets often hold small ETH balances that never move — operational reserves, dust, unused float. PocketChange AI Agent finds those wallets, checks if they're active, and stakes what's safe through Lido at ~3.5% APY. It only stakes what makes sense: inactive wallets, above the dust threshold, when gas won't eat the return. You keep the yield. We take 0.25%.
